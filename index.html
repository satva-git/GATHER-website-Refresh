Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GATHER.nexus.

The GATHER Platform is hosted entirely on Microsoft Azure Cloud, certified and compliant with ISO 27001, SOC 2, and GDPR. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL) and at rest using Azure's built-in encryption. GATHER.nexus is an approved Xero integration partner and undergoes regular security audits. Payment processing is handled by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant). Your data is 100% owned by you and is never shared with any third parties.
GATHER AI is underpinned by Microsoft Azure AI. Our first AI feature enables auto-mapping from underlying Xero or QuickBooks General Ledger accounts to Group Reporting Templates. No customer-specific financial data is shared with the AI engine, only the account name, account ID and account type are shared in encrypted format.
Multi-entity businesses operate across more than one legal entity, common in VC or PE backed scale-ups expanding internationally. GATHER delivers apps across three key areas: Group (Consolidated) Financial Reporting, Group Financial Control (including Intercompany Control), and Group (Consolidated) Financial Planning.
The GATHER Platform is built for Finance teams in businesses with a multi-entity corporate structure integrating with Xero and QuickBooks. Our features particularly benefit Finance teams in venture or PE backed scale-up businesses and Fractional CFOs who work with such businesses.
GATHER.nexus offers seamless integration with Xero and QuickBooks with a single click, allowing you to import and manage your financial data effortlessly across all your legal entities. Users may create Groups of legal entities which are a mixture of Xero and QuickBooks entities.
Users may access a 30-day Free Trial Period, during which they have access to all the features of the GATHER Platform, as well as benefiting from regular new features added to the Platform. No credit card required.
Pricing packages are tailored according to (a) which multi-entity applications you wish to use and (b) how many Groups of companies you wish to manage on the Platform. In each case, users pay a monthly subscription fee. Full details are on our pricing page.
You can upgrade at any time, fees are paid monthly in advance. If you decide to downgrade or cancel, you may do so with 14 days notice and your current plan will elapse at the end of the month in which the notice period completes.
