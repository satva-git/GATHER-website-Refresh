# How to Leave Feedback on GATHER.nexus Website

Welcome! We've set up an easy way for you to review the website and share your feedback directly on the page.

## Quick Steps

1. **Right-click anywhere** on the page
2. Select **"Add comment here"**
3. Enter your name and your feedback
4. Click **"Post comment"**
5. Your comment appears as a **numbered pin** on the page

That's it! 🎉

---

## Understanding the Interface

### Numbered Pins
- Each pin shows your comment number (1, 2, 3, etc.)
- Hover over a pin to see a preview of the comment
- Click a pin to open the full comment thread

### Comment Status
- **Open** (blue badge) — Waiting for a response
- **Resolved** (gray badge) — Addressed and complete

### Comments Panel
- Click **"Comments"** (top-right) to see all feedback at once
- Shows feedback count and allows you to jump between sections
- Helpful if you want to see everything in one view

---

## Leaving Good Feedback

### ✅ Good Examples
- "The pricing table could use better color contrast on the 'most popular' plan"
- "The intercompany control section feels text-heavy; consider adding icons"
- "Suggest making the CTA buttons larger on mobile view"

### ❌ Avoid
- Super vague comments ("looks weird")
- Not enough detail ("change it")
- Feedback on typos (mention specifically: "Line 47: 'syncing' should be 'synced'")

---

## Replying & Conversations

You can see **replies from the designer** directly below your comment:
- Click your comment pin to open the thread
- Read any responses or implementation updates
- Designer may ask clarifying questions

---

## Offline Mode (Comments Saved Locally)

If you see **"Offline"** in the top-left corner:
- ✅ Comments are still being saved
- ✅ They're saved on your computer
- ⚠️ Your comments won't sync to the designer
- Solution: Let the designer know, or try refreshing the page

---

## Tips

- **Be specific:** Say *where* and *what* (not just "fix it")
- **Use the pins:** Hover and click to see the full context of your feedback
- **Check replies:** Designer may have already responded to your comment
- **Proofread:** Click comment preview to confirm before posting
- **Multiple visits:** Your comments stay visible across sessions on this link

---

## Questions or Issues?

- Feedback not posting? Try refreshing the page
- Link expired? Contact the designer for a new one
- Browser issue? Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge all work)

---

## What Happens Next?

1. You leave feedback with right-click comments
2. Designer receives notifications of your comments
3. Designer can reply, mark as resolved, or request clarification
4. You can see all updates and replies in real-time
5. When complete, all feedback is exported for the team
