The challenge

Multi-entity finance breaks when every entity lives in its own silo

Xero and QuickBooks handle entity-level accounting well. Group consolidation, intercompany matching, and consolidated planning still land in spreadsheets — slow, error-prone, and hard to audit.

01

Fragmented ledgers

Each legal entity syncs independently. No shared view of intercompany balances, group P&L, or cash flow until someone manually stitches it together.

02

Month-end drag

Consolidation, eliminations, and IC reconciliation consume days of senior finance time — time that should go to analysis and board reporting.

03

Planning in parallel

Entity budgets live in separate files. Rolling them up to a group forecast means duplicate work and version-control risk before every board cycle.

The solution

One platform from entity ledger to group output

GATHER.nexus sits above your Xero and QuickBooks entities. Data flows in continuously; group reporting, intercompany control, and planning run in connected workflows — not disconnected exports.

Entity data in. Group confidence out.

Connect entities with one click, map accounts with GATHER AI, then operate across three purpose-built modules — each designed for multi-entity groups, not retrofitted from single-entity tools.

How it works

From entity ledger to group output — four steps

GATHER.nexus connects your existing accounting platforms without replacing them. Each step builds on the last inside a single continuous workflow.

01

Connect your entities

One-click integration with each Xero or QuickBooks company. Mix platforms freely — there is no requirement for every entity to run the same software.

02

Map accounts with AI

GATHER AI suggests how each entity's GL accounts map to your Group Reporting Template — with confidence scores. Privacy-safe: only account names and types are transmitted, never transaction data.

03

Control intercompany activity

Post quadruple-entry IC transactions that sync directly to entity ledgers. Track balances with matrix and timeline views. Reconcile against consolidation working papers automatically each period.

04

Consolidate, report, and plan

Run group P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow against your template with IC eliminations applied. Layer entity budgets and scenario forecasts across the same consolidated data structure.
